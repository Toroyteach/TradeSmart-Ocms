Rose Ronoh

Advisory Board Member

Rose possess an extensive experience in trade facilitation, trade policy, cross border trade, regional integration, ICT for trade, stakeholder engagement, change management, customer experience, marketing and communications, she is an enthusiast of the Africa Continental Free 4 Trade Area (AfCFTA) and she is currently the AfCFTA expert in Kenya. Recently she has consulted for GIZ and ACWICT in documenting success stories of cross border women traders at the borders of Namanga, Busia, Malaba and Isebania.
